# 💫 About Me:
Martins is a Geospatial Software Enthusiast with a strong background GIS Remote Sensing. Currently building Vironet, a platform powered by Django, Next JS, Celery & Celery beat, Redis, PostgreSQL, PostGIS, Mapbox GL JS, Tailwind CSS, and more. Vironet is designed to empower the renewable energy community, offering a space for showcasing work and tracking impact in terms of carbon credit.<br>Beyond code, he engages in physical activities such as football, snooker, and maintain a fitness routine at the gym. His commitment to excellence extends to both professional and personal development.<br>Connect with Martins to explore opportunities, share insights, or collaborate on projects at the intersection of technology and sustainable solutions.
